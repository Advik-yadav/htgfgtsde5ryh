Services

Physiotherapy Solutions

Ankle Sprain

We approach ankle sprain treatment with a focus on pain management, swelling reduction, and restoring mobility. Through personalized exercises, strength training, and balance work, we aim to rebuild stability and prevent re-injury.

Shoulder Impingement

As a physiotherapists, we address shoulder impingement by initially focusing on pain management and reducing inflammation through modalities like ice therapy and manual techniques.

Slip Disc

Initially, We focus on pain relief through modalities such as ice therapy and soft tissue mobilization. We then implement stretching and strengthening exercises to address tightness and weakness in the foot and calf muscles.

Osteoarthritis

Our approach osteoarthritis treatment with a focus on pain management, joint mobility, and functional improvement. We use a combination of manual therapy techniques, such as joint mobilizations and soft tissue massage, to alleviate pain and stiffness in affected joints.

Spinal Cord Injuries

Our approach to treating spinal cord injuries (SCI) focuses on maximizing function, independence, and quality of life. We begin with a comprehensive assessment to understand the extent of injury and its impact on mobility, sensation, and daily activities.

Bells Palsy

Approach treating Bell's palsy involves rehabilitation techniques aimed at improving facial muscle strength, mobility, and coordination. We utilize exercises such as facial muscle strengthening, range of motion exercises, and neuromuscular re-education to restore facial symmetry and function.
